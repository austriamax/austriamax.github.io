Tarea 13. Pregunta 17.1.

Construya tres v.a. \(U\), \(V\) y \(W\) tales que \(\mathbb{E}[\mathbb{E}(U|V)|W] \neq \mathbb{E}[\mathbb{E}(U|W)|V]\).

Demostración:

Sea \(([0,1],\mathscr{B}([0,1]), \lambda)\) un espacio de probabilidad con \(\lambda\) la medida de Lebesgue.

Se definen las v.a. \(X\), \(Y\) y \(Z\) como:

\[\begin{align*} U(\omega) &= \omega\\\\ V(\omega) &= 1_{[0,\frac{3}{4}]}(\omega) + v1_{(\frac{3}{4},1]}(\omega)\\\\ W(\omega) &= w1_{[0,\frac{1}{4}]}(\omega) + 1_{[\frac{3}{4},1]}(\omega) \end{align*}\]

Por lo que sus \(\sigma-\)álgebras generadas son:

\[\begin{align*} \sigma(V) &= \{\emptyset, [0, \frac{3}{4}, (\frac{3}{4},1],[0,1]\}.\\ \sigma(W) &= \{\emptyset, [0,\frac{1}{4}], (\frac{1}{4},1], [0,1]\}. \end{align*} \]

Por la medibilidad de \(\mathbb{E}[U|V]\) y por el hecho de que \(V\) solo tenga dos elementos en su soporte, tenemos que:

\[\mathbb{E}[U|V] = a_01_{V=0} + a_11_{V=1} \quad \mbox{con }a_1,a_2 \in \mathbb{R}\]

Además se debe cumplir que \(\forall A \in \sigma(V)\) \[\int_A Vd\lambda = \int_A \mathbb{E}[U|V]d\lambda\] Y la \(\sigma-\)aditividad de \(\lambda\) es suficiente que se cumpla para \(\{v=0\} = (\frac{3}{4},1]\) y \(\{v=1\} = [0,\frac{3}{4}]\). Ahora calcularemos \(a_0\) y \(a_1\):

\[\begin{align*} \int_{A_i}Ud\lambda &= \int_{A_i} \mathbb{E}[U|V]d\lambda\\ &= \int 1_{A_i}[a_01_{A_0} + a_11_{A_1}]d\lambda\\ &= \int a_i 1_{A_i}d\lambda\\ &=a_i \lambda(A_i).\\\\ \therefore a_i &= \frac{1}{\lambda(A_i)}\int_{A_i}Ud\lambda \end{align*} \]

Entonces

\[ \begin{align*} a_0 &= \frac{1}{\lambda(\{V = 0\})}\int_{\{V = 0\}} Ud\lambda\\\\ &= \frac{1}{\lambda((\frac{3}{4},1])}\int_{\frac{3}{4}}^1 \omega d\omega\\\\ &=\frac{1}{\frac{1}{4}}[\frac{\omega^2}{2}]_{\frac{3}{4}}^1\\\\ &= 4(\frac{1}{2} - \frac{9}{32} )\\\\ &= \frac{7}{8} \end{align*} \]

\[ \begin{align*} a_1 &= \frac{1}{\lambda(\{V = 1\})}\int_{\{V = 1\}} Ud\lambda\\\\ &= \frac{1}{\lambda((0,\frac{3}{4}])}\int_{0}^{\frac{3}{4}} \omega d\omega\\\\ &=\frac{4}{3}[\frac{\omega^2}{2}]_{0}^{\frac{3}{4}}\\\\ &= \frac{4}{3}(\frac{9}{32} )\\\\ &= \frac{3}{8} \end{align*} \]

\[\therefore \mathbb{E}[U|V] = \frac{3}{8} 1_{[0,\frac{3}{4}]} + \frac{7}{8}1_{(\frac{3}{4},1]}\]

Dado que \(\mathbb{E}[\mathbb{E}[U|V]|W]\) es \(\sigma\)-medible tenemos que \[\mathbb{E}[\mathbb{E}[U|V]|W] = b_01_{W=0} + b_11_{W = 1} \quad \mbox{para} \quad b_1,b_2\in \mathbb{R}\] con \(\{W = 0\} = [0, \frac{1}{4}]\) y \(\{W = 1\} = (\frac{1}{4},1]\). Al igual que antes se debe cumplir que \[\int_{(W = i)} \mathbb{E}(U|V)d\lambda = \int_{(W = i)} \mathbb{E}[\mathbb{E}(U|V)|W]d\lambda \quad \mbox{para} \quad i=0,1.\] Entonces, por lo ya visto \[ \begin{align*} b_0 &= \frac{1}{\lambda(W = 0)} [\int_{(W = 0)} \mathbb{E}(U|V)d\lambda]\\\\ &= \frac{1}{\lambda([0,\frac{1}{4}])}[\int_0^{\frac{1}{4}}(\frac{3}{8}1_{[0,\frac{3}{4}]}(\omega) + \frac{7}{8}1_{(\frac{3}{4},1]}(\omega))d\omega]\\\\ &= \frac{1}{\frac{1}{4}}[\int_{\mathbb{R}}\frac{3}{8} 1_{[0,\frac{1}{4}]}(\omega)d\omega]\\\\ &=4(\frac{3}{8})\lambda([0,\frac{1}{4}])\\\\ &=\frac{3}{8}. \end{align*} \]

\[ \begin{align*} b_0 &= \frac{1}{\lambda(W = 1)} [\int_{(W = 1)} \mathbb{E}(U|V)d\lambda]\\\\ &= \frac{1}{\lambda((\frac{1}{4},1])}[\int_{\frac{1}{4}}^1(\frac{3}{8}1_{[0,\frac{3}{4}]}(\omega) + \frac{7}{8}1_{(\frac{3}{4},1]}(\omega))d\omega]\\\\ &= \frac{1}{\frac{3}{4}}[\int_{\mathbb{R}}\frac{3}{8} 1_{(\frac{1}{4},\frac{3}{4}]}(\omega)d\omega + \frac{7}{8}\int_\mathbb{R} 1_{[\frac{3}{4},1]}(\omega)d\omega]\\\\ &= \frac{4}{3}[\frac{3}{8}(\frac{1}{2})+ \frac{7}{8}(\frac{1}{4})]\\\\ &= \frac{13}{24} \end{align*} \]

\[\therefore \mathbb{E}[\mathbb{E}[U|V]|W] = \frac{3}{8}1_{[0,\frac{1}{4}]} + \frac{13}{24}1_{(\frac{1}{4},1]}.\]

Ahora repetimos los calculos para \(\mathbb{E}[U|W]\).

\[\mathbb{E}[U|W] = d_01_{(W=0)} + d_11_{(W = 1)}\]

Calculando \(d_0\):

\[\begin{align*} d_0 &= \frac{1}{\lambda(W = 0)} \int_{\{W = 0\}}Ud\lambda\\\\ &= \frac{1}{\lambda([0,\frac{1}{4}])}\int_0^{\frac{1}{4}}\omega d\omega\\\\ &= 4 [\frac{\omega^2}{2}]_0^{\frac{1}{4}}\\\\ &= \frac{1}{8}. \end{align*}\]

Calculando \(d_1\):

\[\begin{align*} d_1 &= \frac{1}{\lambda(W = 1)} \int_{\{W = 1\}}Ud\lambda\\\\ &= \frac{1}{\lambda((\frac{1}{4}, 1])}\int_{\frac{1}{4}}^1\omega d\omega\\\\ &= \frac{4}{3} [\frac{\omega^2}{2}]_{\frac{1}{4}}^1\\\\ &= \frac{4}{3}(\frac{1}{2} - \frac{1}{32})\\\\ &= \frac{15}{24}. \end{align*}\]

\[\therefore \mathbb{E}[U|W] = \frac{1}{8}1_{[0,\frac{1}{4}]} + \frac{15}{24}1_{[\frac{1}{4},1]}\]

Ahora vamos a encontrar las constantes de \[\mathbb{E}[\mathbb{E}[U|W]|V] = c_01_{(V=0)} + c_11_{(V=1)}\]

Calculando \(c_0\):

\[\begin{align*} c_0 &= \frac{1}{\lambda(V=0)}\int_{(V= 0)}\mathbb{E}[U|W]d\lambda\\\\ &= \frac{1}{\lambda((\frac{3}{4},1])}\int 1_{(\frac{3}{4},1]}[\frac{1}{8}1_{[0,\frac{1}{4}]}(\omega) + \frac{15}{24}1_{(\frac{1}{4},1]}(\omega)]d\omega\\\\ &= 4[\frac{15}{24}\int 1_{(\frac{3}{4},1]}(\omega)d\omega]\\\\ &= \frac{15}{6}\lambda((\frac{3}{4},1])\\\\ &=\frac{15}{24}. \end{align*}\]

Calculando \(c_1\):

\[\begin{align*} c_1 &= \frac{1}{\lambda(V=1)} \int_{(V=1)} \mathbb{E}[U|V]d\lambda\\\\ &= \frac{1}{\lambda([0,\frac{3}{4}])}\int 1_{[0,\frac{3}{4}]}[\frac{1}{8}1_{[0,\frac{1}{4}]}(\omega) + \frac{15}{24}1_{(\frac{1}{4},1]}(\omega)]d\omega\\\\ &= \frac{4}{3}[\frac{1}{8}\int 1_{[0,\frac{1}{4}]}(\omega)d\omega + \int 1_{(\frac{1}{4},\frac{3}{4}]}(\omega)d\omega]\\\\ &= \frac{4}{3}\{\frac{1}{8}\lambda([0,\frac{1}{4}])+\frac{15}{24}\lambda((\frac{1}{4},\frac{3}{4}])\}\\\\ &=\frac{1}{24} + \frac{5}{12}\\\\ &= \frac{11}{24} \end{align*}\]

\[\therefore \mathbb{E}[\mathbb{E}[U|W]|V] = \frac{15}{24}1_{(\frac{3}{4},1]} + \frac{11}{24}1_{[0,\frac{3}{4}]}\]

Por lo tanto:

\[\mathbb{E}[\mathbb{E}[U|W]|V] \neq \mathbb{E}[\mathbb{E}[U|W]|V].\]
